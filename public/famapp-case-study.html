PAGE 04 // FRICTION MATRIX

Core Problem Statement

Adoption Barriers

Onboarding Challenges

18+ users face an unnecessarily complex registration funnel. Redundant document requests cause high drop-offs prior to KYC completion.

Brand Awareness Limits

Many potential users remain unaware of FamApp's unique feature benefits (like numberless cards), hindering compounding peer viral loops.

Age Bracket Disconnect

A single messaging model fails to distinguish between younger teens (16-17) seeking pocket-money fun, and older youth (18-20) seeking formal credit tools.

Trust & Security Doubts

Complex minor accounts and rigorous identity/parental validations scare away privacy-conscious parents.

Uniform GTM Mismatch

A uniform user acquisition strategy neglects regional, social, and functional differences across colleges and schools.

Poor Platform Stability

Frequent OTP transmission delays, app freezes, and sign-in errors trigger immediate drop-offs during highly critical first impressions.

PAGE 07 // JOURNEY DIAGNOSTICS

Major Roadblocks & Drop-off Points

Onboarding Funnel
01

Phone SMS Verify

Parental Hurdle: Parents are busy, hesitant to share their critical credentials, or lack digital know-how.

02

Basic Details

Doc Friction: Teens lack rapid access to documents. Aadhaar linkage to a different mobile number triggers immediate blockages.

03

Source Funding

Tech Drops: App freezes on lower bandwidth or fails during multi-stage bank-redirect steps, forcing permanent abandonment.

04

Aadhaar KYC

No Instant Hook: Users sign up out of curiosity but abandon the flow because there's no immediate reward to fund and transact.

05

Selfie verify

"Now What?" Loop: Path to add initial deposit re-introduces parental authorization screens, killing autonomous utility.

06

Add Email / Finish

Value Prop Gap: High-friction KYC finishes without presenting any "wow" features or special offers, offering little incentive to return.

PAGE 08 // RECOMMENDATION GRID

Age-Segmented Product Recommendations

Minor vs Major

🧒 Minor Users — Below 18 Years

1. Daily Use Case Expansion

Add metro/bus ticket booking and digital transit card integrations to make FamApp a daily utility.

2. Financial Literacy Hub

Introduce gamified lessons and quizzes within the app to bypass rigid investment regulations safely.

3. Peer Group Expense Splits

Integrate smart in-app group splitting and settlements to spark organic peer recommendations.

🎓 Major Users — 18 Years & Over

1. Frictionless Phone Signup

Direct phone signup with optional wallet activation to replace the mandatory ₹99 KYC fee with partner rewards.

2. Credit & Utility Enablement

Add loan repayment channels, personal credit options, and utility bill splits to boost daily retention.

3. Co-Branded Cards

Launch premium credit and debit card partnerships with major retail banks to lock in long-term loyalty.

PAGE 09 // GTM MATRIX

Go-To-Market Execution Blueprint

Proposed Proposals
FamZones

Student Privilege Zones

Establish deal zones around major college cafes with instant group discount rewards. Repositions FamApp from a standard UPI utility to a core student lifestyle companion.

Spark Grant

Angel for Dreams

Introduce a ₹1 Lakh monthly grant for student projects, allowing teams to enter via Reels and vote in-app. Positions the brand as an investor in students' futures, building organic advocacy.

Fest-Pay

College Fest Wallet

Become the exclusive digital wallet partner for university festivals, providing express entry lanes and fast checkout rewards. Drives rapid, high-density on-campus adoption over 2-3 days.

Squad Wallet

Easy Group Spending

Add friends-pool shared balances with automated transaction logging. Solves the friction of separate split utilities, driving repeating active daily usage and strong social group lock-in.
