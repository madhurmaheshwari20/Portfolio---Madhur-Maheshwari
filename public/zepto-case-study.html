PAGE 05 // SYSTEM BLUEPRINT

The Execution Cliff: Resolving Sizing & Logistics Risks

Core Strategy Matrice
🔄

Eliminating "Return Burn"

Strategy A Doorstep Monetization

The "Instant-Fit" Protocol

Delivery riders facilitate quick doorstep try-on. Compensated with brief convenience wait fees to secure driver efficiency. Conversions instantly protected.

Core Metric: Converts failed sizes into immediate doorstep returns (Saves reverse logistic loops).

Strategy B Friction Locking

Cross-Brand "True-Size" Mapping

Referencing custom body profiling comparing shopper choices in global brands (Zara, H&M) to programmatically align predictions directly during shopping.

Effect: Cuts initial listing size returns by over 35% on checkout.

🏬

Neutralizing "Inventory Burn"

Strategy C Logistical Intelligence

Superstore Replenishment (Hub-and-Spoke)

Dark stores keep strictly 2–4 hours of hyper-local visual trend clothing blocks. Central warehouses feed local hubs of high-velocity stock three times daily.

Effect: Achieves major square-foot sales productivity in prime metropolitan areas.

Strategy D P&L Protection

Zero-Risk Consignment (SOR model)

Incentivizing partner labels with a Sale-or-Return workflow. Retained inventory risk remains brand-owned, removing reverse asset burden on the Balance Sheet.

Capital Preservation: Zero-overhead storage risks and limitless styling options.
